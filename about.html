Our Mission

EcoOps is a cloud-native sustainability project built on Red Hat OpenShift. Our mission is to make DevOps greener and more efficient by combining cloud scalability with environmentally conscious deployment strategies.

Our Team

- DevOps Engineers: Automate CI/CD and deployment pipelines.
- Cloud Engineers: Manage OpenShift clusters and resources.
- Security Analysts: Ensure application and infrastructure protection.
- GreenOps Advocates: Promote energy-efficient operations.

Our Vision

Building a sustainable cloud ecosystem that reduces carbon footprint while delivering performance, security, and innovation.
